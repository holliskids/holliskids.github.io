Cycle the Continental Divide

July 9 - 14, 2018

  • Dates: July 9-14, 2018
  • Cost: $1,300
  • Basic Requirements:Happy attitude, bike, helmet, intermediate+ experience cycling
  • Route:"We’ll travel from the lively resort town of Whitefish, located near Glacier National Park, to the southern end of the spectacular Swan Range, one of Montana’s less-visited geologic gems. Riding mostly on U.S. Forest Service roads and a bit of singletrack, we’ll experience the high country of one of the world’s great wildland complexes. Along the way, we’ll encounter a variety of flora and fauna. We’ll skirt the southern boundaries of the Bob Marshall and Scapegoat wilderness areas and crest the Continental Divide on a loop near Lincoln, our ultimate destination." (Adventure Cycling Association)
